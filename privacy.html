Privacy Policy

Here is our info on our Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aerospace's Privacy Policy, This outlines how Aerospace collects, uses, and protects user data while providing it's most amazing features on discord! By using Aerospace you automatically agree to the info in this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection, Usage, Storage, and Retention

Aerospace collects the following types of data:

  • User Commands: Commands entered by users to interact with the bot
  • Guild Information: Data about the Servers where the bot is added, such as server ID, channel names, and roles
  • User Identifiers: such as user IDs, usernames, and discriminator numbers necessary for bot functionality

Aerospace uses this data to respond to user commands, enhance features, monitor crashes through error logs, and allow our beta testers and developers to identify and fix issues.

We store data temporarily for 2 to 4 weeks. You may request data deletion by DMing the server owner ziggy._.mc. It may take a few days depending on request volume.

Note: Aerospace does NOT collect or store sensitive personal information unless explicitly required and consented to by the user.

3. Third-Party Services

Aerospace uses third-party services like MongoDB for database hosting. These services also comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and accepted data protection standards.

4. Children's Privacy

Aerospace is intended for use on Discord, which requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy and Contact

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be announced in our support server’s announcement channel. Continued use of Aerospace after updates means you agree to them. For questions or deletion requests, use the /support command in our server.
